Make a website with html.

Its good practice to navigate first to the student archive of FabAcademy. You can find lodes of information from students that have already studied the subject and documented their work.

Hint: you can use html5up.net to find layouts that are ready to be deployed for non-comercial use.

I suggest finding good videos on youtube on html programming or alternatively www.w3schools.com to find layouts that are ready to be deployed for non-comercial use.

Your website should be your portfolio and a project diary. Do not worry about formatting or layout. You should instead focus your time on:

  • Pictures
  • Descriptive and perhaps timestamped text with useful resources
  • Design files
  • Any difficulty you encountered/how you solved them
  • Keywords you googled
