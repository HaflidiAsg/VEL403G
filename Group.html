Final project task management
   
    
        1. Make a group site webspage. Its ok to share a common design, but assign and display individual work
        2. Describe how you are going to manage your project; project management software, task trees, etc
        3. Display group photo and individual "roles" if appropriate
   


Assignment completion checklist
   
    
        1. Have you detailed where you found inspiration for your project
        2. Have you described how your group reached consensus on project proposal
        3. Have you planned ahead on when and where you will use relevant machines
        4. Have you determine who will design what and documented thought process
        5. Have you made a rough proposal sketch and posted a picture
