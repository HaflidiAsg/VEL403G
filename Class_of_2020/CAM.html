Final project - Make something big with the CNC 

Assignment - Make something big
   
    
        1. Use a CAD software to build a model you can machine with the shopbot
        2. Test runout, alignment, speeds, feeds and toolpaths
        3. Assemble your invention
   

   
Assignment completion checklist
   
    
        1. Have you made your own model and documented the design principles
        2. Have you described problems and how you fixed them
        3. Have you shown how you made something big, i.e. machine setup, fixings, testing joints, adjusting feeds and speeds, depth of cut etc.
        4. Have you included design files and hero shots

   

   
Expected FAQ:
   
    
        1. What materials can I use? 
            A: About 1,5m x 1,5m x 10mm plywood will be supplied per team of 3 individuals
        2. How much time does it take to machine?
            A: Expect 1 hour after all design files are ready.
        3. How can I make sure my design files are ready?
            A: Hafliði and Arnar will review CAD files prior to booking a time slot in the lab with TA
