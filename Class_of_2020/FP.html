Final Project 

Assignment 7 - Final Project
   
    
        1. Plan a final project that integrates some of the course subjects
        2. Your project should integrate some 2D and 3D elements
        3. Determine an intellectual property licensing
   

Assignment completion checklist
   
    
        1. Have you made one-pager/slide with your names, project names, photo of your masterpiece and a brief description of your project
        2. Have you made a ~3 minute video (1080p on youtube/vimeo) where your project is summarized
        3. Have you made a BOM (Bill of materials) for your project
        4. Have you linked relevant sources from where you got inspiration
        5. Have you included a license of your chosing
        6. Have you documented how you made your project, relevant design files, etc
        7. Have you described project management progression and what you eventually did not do
        8. Have you detailed spiral development, serial/parallel tasks, demand and supply chain management etc.
        9. Have you acknowledged work done by others
