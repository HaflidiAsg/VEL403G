Syllabus 

At the end of the semester students should have be able to:

        - Build an online portfolio of their work and abilities.
        - Have a workflow for digital fabrication that has documentation at its core.
        - Have an organized version control of their work.
        - Be able to organize groupwork in a professional manner with project management methods.
        - Understand various project managmenet principles for design and development work.
        - Design and work with various digitally produced 2D and 3D elements for cutting, engraving and milling.
        - Understand various design principles.
        - Operate a laser cutter, vinyl cutter, 3D printer and CNC machines for practical purposes.
        - Have an understanding of integrating fabrication methods for making.
        - Present their work regularly.
        - Understand the concept of intellectual property.
